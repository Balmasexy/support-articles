Overview:

Attempts to add a new trigger (i.e, scheduled pipeline) to your project settings may fail with the error:

Failed to save scheduled Trigger,Try Again

This is due to the maximum number of triggers (for a given project) being reached.

By default, this limit is: 50

 

Solution:

Please contact CircleCI Support to request an increase of the aforementioned limit.
