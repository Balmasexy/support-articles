Overview

You may need to generate logs as part of your troubleshooting process when using Self-Hosted Runners . 

 

Where can I find logs? 

On Windows, you can find self-hosted runner logs in the following location: 

C:\ProgramFiles\CircleCI\circleci-runner.log
